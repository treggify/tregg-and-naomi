Our Story

How God brought two moongazers together.
From classmates, to travel buddies, to girlfriend and boyfriend, to future husband and wife.

  • January 2015

    Photography Class

    Tregg and Naomi had a digital photography course together in the spring semester of 2015. Once he learned that Naomi was going to Japan as well, every day Tregg came into class he would ask if she had purcahsed her tickets yet.

  • May 2015

    Dinner with a friend

    Naomi and Tregg had their first dinner together with a mutual friend. That friend got up to use the restroom leaving them alone together. They twiddled their thumbs awkwardly and watched the TVs in the restaurant as they waited for their friend to return.

  • June 4th, 2015

    Japan.

    Naomi greeted Tregg with a (much needed) huge hug in LA after a long delay and tiring flight from Denver. While sitting on the airplane in LA, waiting to head to Tokyo, he sent Naomi a text message about the hand towels they had been handed by the flight attendants. He asserts this was not intentional flirting, but she thinks otherwise.

  • June 16th, 2015

    A New Moon / The First Kiss

    After 12 days of long walks, museum visits, and design studio talks, Tregg and Naomi kissed in Kyoto, Japan. It's important to note that this occurred on a New Moon.

  • June 26th, 2015

    The Relationship Begins

    Once they were back in the states, they took two days to make the relationship official.

  • July 1st, 2015

    A Full Moon / "I love you."

    Tregg and Naomi say "I love you". (Things are moving fast!) After learning that they both had the same habit of looking at the moon and thinking "My future spouse is looking at the same moon I am." throughout their youth, they knew it was destiny. This happened on a Full Moon.

  • June 26th, 2016

    The Engagement

    After a year of being in love, Tregg got on one knee and proposed to the love of his life, Naomi.
